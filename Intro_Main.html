About Us

We’re Natalie and Eddie Sharaga, married for 18 wonderful years and proud parents to three amazing children: Arielle (17), Rianne (15), and Dean (12). Together, we’ve built a close-knit family grounded in mutual respect, curiosity, and joy. Our home is filled with laughter, learning, and shared experiences—whether we’re exploring new places or simply enjoying quiet evenings together.

As a family, we love experiences that engage our minds and bring us closer together. One of our favorite things to do is solve escape rooms—whether in person or virtual. We enjoy challenging our brains, working as a team, and celebrating the little victories that come with cracking a tough puzzle.

We also love traveling together and experiencing new places, foods, and cultures. Whether we’re watching a favorite TV series, hiking outdoors, or playing board games on a rainy afternoon, our best memories are the ones we create together. We value connection, shared experiences, and making the most of the time we spend as a family.
